Toby and Lindsay

First meeting at Cardiff University, Toby and Lindsay were dating for nearly 8 years before Toby finally popped the question much to the relief of everyone around them it seems.

After a spell of living in Chislehurst near London, Toby and Lindsay returned westward to settle in Historic Bath™, and have since bought a house overlooking the city center, providing excellent views of hot air balloons during the summer months.
