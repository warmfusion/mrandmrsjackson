Toby and Lindsay

Quite frankly it’s a miracle we’re getting married at all!

We are lucky enough to live in the beautiful Historic Bath™, in our own wee house overlooking the city centre (up a big hill!), which is ideal for spotting hot air balloons in the summer months (a favourite pastime of ours).
